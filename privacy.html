Privacy Policy for Zentry

Effective Date: 2025-06-01

Zentry ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application ("the App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Zentry is designed to function entirely offline. At this time:

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.
  • We do not use analytics tools or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect location, contact, health, or device-specific information.
  • No data is sent to servers or external services.

2. Data You Enter

The app allows you to enter activities, notes, and set timers or reminders. This information:

  • Is stored locally on your device only.
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties.
  • Can be deleted by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

3. Data Security

While Zentry does not store personal data remotely, we recommend using your device’s built-in security features, such as encryption or screen lock, to protect your information.

4. Your Rights

GDPR (EU Residents)

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Zentry does not process or store personal data.

CCPA (California Residents)

  • You have the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion.
  • Zentry does not collect personal information and is exempt from CCPA data collection obligations.

5. Future Updates

If Zentry introduces cloud syncing, motion tracking, or online services, this policy will be updated accordingly and you will be notified.
